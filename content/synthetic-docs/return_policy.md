# Return Policy

## Purpose

This fictional return policy tells support agents when retail customers may return Nura Wellness products. It applies only to orders placed on the Nura storefront and does not apply to wholesale, marketplace, affiliate, or retailer purchases.

Support should use this policy together with the product catalog, allergen policy, and support escalation SOP when a customer reports discomfort or a safety concern.

## Standard Return Window

Retail customers may request a return within 30 days of delivery. Unopened products may be returned for a refund to the original payment method, excluding original shipping fees unless the company made a fulfillment error.

Opened products may be returned within 30 days when the customer tried the product and is unsatisfied with taste, texture, or general experience. Support may approve one opened product return per order without manager approval if the customer provides the order number and reason.

## Discomfort Or Adverse Experience

If a customer says a product caused mild stomach discomfort, headache, or other non-severe discomfort, support should avoid medical interpretation. Support may explain the return option if the request is within 30 days and should recommend stopping use until the customer speaks with a qualified professional if they are concerned.

If the customer reports severe symptoms such as trouble breathing, swelling, chest pain, fainting, or emergency care, support must follow the escalation SOP. The refund conversation should not replace the safety escalation.

## Non-Returnable Orders

Final-sale bundles, free gifts, expired products purchased from third-party sellers, and orders outside the 30-day window are not eligible for standard returns. Wholesale orders are governed by separate agreements and must be escalated.

Products damaged by the customer after delivery are not eligible for a standard refund. If carrier damage is reported, support should follow the shipping policy.

## Refund Processing

Approved refunds are submitted after the returned item is received or after a manager approves a no-return exception. Refunds usually appear within 5 to 10 business days after processing, depending on the customer's bank.

Support should document the reason for every refund and include whether the item was opened, unopened, damaged, or related to discomfort.
