# Health Claims Compliance

## Purpose

Nura Wellness sells fictional dietary supplements, not medicines. Support agents must avoid disease claims and medical advice. All answers should stay within approved product facts, label directions, and company policies.

The safest answer is often a limited answer. If the documents do not support a claim, the assistant and support agents should say that the company documents do not provide enough information.

## Prohibited Claims

Support agents must not say that any supplement cures, treats, diagnoses, prevents, or reverses a disease or medical condition. This includes claims about anxiety, depression, insomnia, arthritis, diabetes, high blood pressure, hormone disorders, infections, or digestive diseases.

Support agents must not interpret symptoms, recommend dosing changes for a medical condition, or advise a customer to stop or start a medication. If a customer asks whether a supplement is safe for a diagnosed condition, pregnancy, breastfeeding, a child, or medication use, support should recommend speaking with a qualified professional.

## Allowed Structure And Function Language

Support may use approved structure and function language from the product catalog. For example, Magnesium Glycinate Calm may be described as supporting normal muscle function and relaxation as part of a balanced lifestyle. Vitamin D3 may be described as supporting normal immune function and bone health.

Allowed statements must not be turned into disease claims. "Supports relaxation" is acceptable when copied from approved copy. "Treats anxiety" is not acceptable. "Supports immune function" is acceptable. "Prevents infections" is not acceptable.

## Customer-Facing Guidance

When a customer asks a medical question, support should answer with empathy, state that Nura cannot provide medical advice, and direct the customer to a qualified professional. If the customer reports a severe reaction, support should follow the escalation SOP immediately.

If a customer asks whether a product will work for them, support may explain the intended use and label directions but must avoid guarantees. The phrase "results vary" may be used when paired with approved product information.

## Review And Escalation

Potentially non-compliant claims in customer messages, reviews, ads, or influencer content should be escalated to Compliance Review. Support must not edit, approve, or reuse customer testimonials that imply disease treatment.
