# Product Catalog

## Purpose

This fictional product catalog gives support agents approved product facts for Nura Wellness. It is the primary source for product names, serving sizes, intended use language, and basic allergen notes.

Support may answer product questions from this catalog, but medical questions must follow the health claims compliance policy. If a product fact is missing, support should say the documents do not provide enough information.

## Magnesium Glycinate Calm

Magnesium Glycinate Calm is a capsule product for adults. The serving size is two capsules, providing 200 mg magnesium as magnesium glycinate. Approved language says it supports normal muscle function and relaxation as part of a balanced lifestyle.

The product does not intentionally contain milk, soy, gluten, peanuts, tree nuts, fish, shellfish, or sesame. It is made in a shared facility that also processes major allergens. The bottle contains 60 capsules, or 30 servings.

## Vanilla Plant Protein

Vanilla Plant Protein is a powdered supplement for adults. One serving is two scoops mixed with water or a beverage of choice. Approved language says it provides a convenient source of plant-based protein for active lifestyles.

The formula contains pea protein, rice protein, natural vanilla flavor, sunflower lecithin, and monk fruit extract. It does not intentionally contain milk or soy, but it is produced in a shared facility. The pouch contains 20 servings.

## Daily D3 Softgels

Daily D3 Softgels provide 1000 IU vitamin D3 per softgel. Approved language says Vitamin D3 supports normal immune function and bone health. The product is intended for adults who want a simple daily vitamin D supplement.

Support must not say that Daily D3 treats deficiency or prevents illness. Customers who are pregnant, breastfeeding, taking medication, or managing a medical condition should consult a qualified professional before use.

## Daily Greens Citrus

Daily Greens Citrus is a flavored powder for adults. It includes wheatgrass, barley grass, spirulina, chlorella, and citrus bioflavonoids. Approved language says it supports daily nutrient intake as part of a balanced diet.

The product is not a meal replacement and should not be described as a detox, cleanse, or treatment. Customers with gluten concerns should review the allergen policy and product label before use.
