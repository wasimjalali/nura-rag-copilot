# Subscription Policy

## Purpose

This fictional subscription policy explains renewals, cancellations, skips, and subscription discounts for Nura Wellness. It applies only to subscriptions created on the Nura storefront.

Support agents should be clear that a subscription is optional and that customers can manage it from their account portal before renewal.

## Renewal Timing

Subscriptions renew on the schedule selected by the customer at checkout, usually every 30, 45, or 60 days. Renewal reminders are sent by email three days before the renewal date when the customer has opted into email notifications.

Once a renewal order is processed, support cannot guarantee cancellation of that order. If the customer contacts support immediately after renewal, agents should check fulfillment status before promising any change.

## Cancellation

Customers may cancel future subscription renewals from the account portal or by contacting support before the renewal is processed. Cancellation stops future renewals but does not automatically refund an order that has already processed.

If a customer says they tried to cancel before renewal but the portal failed, support should review account notes and timestamps. Manager approval is required for refund exceptions after renewal.

## Skips And Pauses

Customers may skip one upcoming renewal or pause a subscription for up to 90 days. Support agents may help with one skip or pause request without manager approval when the request is made before renewal processing.

Subscriptions paused for more than 90 days should be cancelled instead of held indefinitely. Customers can restart later by placing a new subscription order.

## Subscription Discounts

Subscription discounts apply only to active subscription orders. They cannot be stacked with one-time promotional codes, wholesale pricing, or manual goodwill credits unless a manager approves an exception.

If a customer cancels and later re-subscribes, the currently published subscription discount applies. Support must not promise expired promotional rates.
