# Shipping Policy

## Purpose

This fictional shipping policy explains shipping timelines, carrier issues, and address handling for Nura Wellness orders. Support agents should use it when customers ask where an order is, whether an order can be changed, or how international delivery works.

Shipping timelines are estimates and not guarantees. Support should avoid promising a delivery date unless the carrier tracking page confirms it.

## Domestic Shipping

Orders shipping within the United States usually leave the warehouse within 1 to 2 business days. Standard delivery usually takes 3 to 6 business days after the carrier receives the package. Expedited delivery usually takes 2 to 3 business days after carrier pickup.

Warehouse processing does not occur on weekends or fictional company holidays. A customer who orders late Friday should expect processing to begin the next business day.

## International Shipping

Nura ships to Canada, Germany, France, the Netherlands, and Sweden in this fictional policy. International orders usually leave the warehouse within 2 business days and usually arrive within 7 to 14 business days after carrier pickup.

Customers are responsible for customs duties, import taxes, and local compliance restrictions. Support may provide tracking information but must not guarantee customs clearance or advise customers on import law.

## Address Changes

Support may update the shipping address before the warehouse creates the shipping label. Once a label is created, support cannot guarantee address changes. The customer may need to contact the carrier directly.

If an order is returned because of an incorrect address, support may reship once after collecting a corrected address. Refunds for incorrect-address returns exclude original shipping fees unless the company caused the address error.

## Lost Or Damaged Packages

A domestic package may be reviewed as potentially lost if tracking has not updated for 7 business days. An international package may be reviewed after 14 business days without tracking movement.

If a package arrives damaged, support should request photos of the package and product. Agents may offer a replacement shipment when the damage is visible and the order is within 30 days of delivery.
